Privacy Policy

CafLog – Caffeine Tracker  ·  Solstice Digital Apps  ·  Effective: June 2025

1. Overview

CafLog is designed with privacy as a core principle. All data you enter is stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. CafLog does not require account registration, email address, or any identifying information.

All information you enter into the app — drink logs, timestamps, and settings — is stored only in your device's local storage (localStorage). This data never leaves your device.

3. Advertising

The free version of CafLog may display banner advertisements served by Google AdMob. Google may use cookies and device identifiers to show relevant ads. You can opt out of personalised advertising in your device's Google settings. The paid "Remove Ads" purchase permanently disables all advertising within the app.

For more information on how Google uses data, see: policies.google.com/privacy

4. Permissions

CafLog does not request access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, or any sensitive device features.

5. Children

CafLog is not directed at children under 13 years of age and does not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: solsticeapps@proton.me
